Latitude Vs Cloudiness

Latitude Vs Cloudiness

Cloudiness Map

The above graph is plotting the percentage of cloudiness at the observed city's latitudes.

From the 560 cities we analyzed in this study, we are not showing a correlation between Cloudiness and distance to the equator. Our study does not find cities that are closer to the equator to be more or less cloudy than those futher away from the equator. It appears that many of the cities in our study were either very clear or very cloudy, but that does not correlate to their distance to the equator.
